The Navigator Stars are suppousedly a race of sentient celestial beings that roam the skies above many different worlds. They seemingly exist only as a legend, as nobody has documented seeing one. The stars were used as the first form of navigation, and the Navigator Stars (hence their name) are no different. They exist to guide heroes to save worlds in danger, and its up to them to pick the right person for the job. Do they exist? Nobody knows, but its likely that the multiverse would be a much worse place if they didn't. My bets are on the fact that they do exist, and possibly are still sending heroes to this day.
